## FAQ

### What does SpendLens actually audit?

SpendLens compares your entered AI tool plans, seats, and monthly spend against public vendor pricing. It identifies possible plan mismatches, overlapping tools, and API or credit spend worth reviewing.

### Does SpendLens know my final bill is wrong?

No. Public pricing is a benchmark, not a billing statement. Your bill may include add-ons, taxes, negotiated terms, or intentional extra usage, so every recommendation is presented as something to verify.

### Why do you ask for email after the result?

The useful result should come first. Email is optional after the audit and is used to send the report or follow up on a requested Credex credit review.

### Will my email or company name appear on the public report?

No. Public share pages show the tools and savings result only. Lead details are stored separately and are not loaded into shared reports.

### When is a Credex credit review useful?

It is most relevant when a team is repeatedly buying AI credits or the audit shows a large monthly opportunity. The review is meant to verify whether the same useful AI usage can cost less, not to force teams to stop using tools that help them ship.
